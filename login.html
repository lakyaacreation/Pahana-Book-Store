Help Section

System usage guidelines to ensure correct operation

Retrieving Customer Details

  • • Enter the customer's account number in the search field
  • • Click "Search" to retrieve customer information
  • • Review customer details before proceeding with billing
  • • Account numbers are typically 6-8 digits long

Calculating Bills

  • • Enter the customer's account number
  • • Input previous and current meter readings
  • • Verify the rate per unit (default: $0.15)
  • • Click "Calculate Bill" to get the total amount
  • • Double-check all readings before finalizing

Printing Bills

  • • Enter customer account number and bill amount
  • • Select the appropriate payment method
  • • Click "Generate Receipt" to create the bill
  • • Review the receipt before printing
  • • Provide copy to customer after payment

Important Notes

  • • Always verify customer identity before processing
  • • Keep backup records of all transactions
  • • Report any system errors immediately
  • • Ensure printer has sufficient paper and ink
  • • Contact admin for technical support
